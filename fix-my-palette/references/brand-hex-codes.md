## HOW TO USE THIS REFERENCE

1. **Client says "something like Spotify"** → note the differentiation strategy (uncrowded color) + exact hex (#1DB954) + tone (vibrant, modern, growth)
2. **Client says "professional and trustworthy"** → recommend blue family. Show them LinkedIn (#0A66C2), IBM (#006699), PayPal (#003087) as reference range
3. **Client needs differentiation** → audit competitor list first → find their dominant color → recommend opposite end of spectrum
4. **Client wants luxury** → Black/white mono (Apple, Chanel, Nike) or deep purple (Cadbury, Twitch) or gold accent (Visa, Gucci, Louis Vuitton)
5. **Client wants approachable/friendly** → Orange (Amazon, Headspace), warm yellow (McDonald's, Snapchat), or coral (Airbnb)
